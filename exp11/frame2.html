About college

Government college of Technology coimbatore,shortly called Gct Coimbatore.Gct is a no.1 engineering college among the all other Government engineering colleges.

Mission

The mission of the college is as follows: To provide the students with knowledge, skills, values and sensitivity to face the challenges in life both in academic field as well as in their personal life. To develop the human potential to achieve different opportunities in future.



Vision

To be a center of excellence in higher education with an innovative teaching, learning, research activities, to cater to the academic needs of the students by providing qualitative education making them self sufficient in life by following holistic approach for the growth of their interpersonal and intrapersonal skills which are essential to build a creative, enlightened and productive civil society in this remote hinterland.
