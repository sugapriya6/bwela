Space exploration is the investigation of outer space through human and robotic missions. It involves studying celestial bodies, understanding cosmic phenomena, and developing technology for interstellar travel. Over the years, space agencies like NASA, ESA, and ISRO have launched numerous missions to explore planets, moons, and asteroids. Spacecraft like the Voyager probes, Mars rovers, and the James Webb Telescope have revolutionized our knowledge of the universe. With advancements in technology, future missions aim at colonizing Mars, exploring exoplanets, and even interstellar travel. Space tourism is also becoming a reality, making space more accessible to humans than ever before.
