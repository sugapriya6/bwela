🌀 Jupiter - The Gas Giant

Jupiter is the **largest planet** in our solar system and is known for its **Great Red Spot**, a massive storm that has been raging for centuries. It is a **gas giant** primarily composed of **hydrogen and helium** and has **79 known moons**, including the four largest: **Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto**.

🌌 Key Facts About Jupiter

  • 🌞 **Orbit Duration**: 11.86 Earth years
  • ⏳ **Rotation Period**: 9.93 hours (fastest spinning planet)
  • ❄️ **Surface Temperature**: -145°C (-234°F)
  • ☁️ **Atmosphere**: 90% hydrogen, 10% helium
  • 🌕 **Moons**: 79 (including Europa, Ganymede, Callisto, Io)

🚀 Space Exploration of Jupiter

  • 🛰️ **Pioneer 10 & 11** (1973-74) – First flybys of Jupiter
  • 🛰️ **Voyager 1 & 2** (1979) – Detailed images of Jupiter and its moons
  • 🛰️ **Galileo Probe** (1995-2003) – First orbiter around Jupiter
  • 🛰️ **Juno Mission** (2016 - Present) – Studying Jupiter’s atmosphere and magnetic field
  • 🚀 **Future Mission**: **Europa Clipper (2024)** – Exploring Jupiter’s moon Europa for signs of life
