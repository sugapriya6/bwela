🌍 Earth - Our Home Planet

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to support **life**. It has a vast diversity of landscapes, including mountains, oceans, forests, and deserts. **70% of Earth's surface** is covered in water, and its atmosphere is composed mainly of **nitrogen (78%) and oxygen (21%)**, making it perfect for sustaining life.

🌌 Key Facts About Earth

  • 🌞 **Orbit Duration**: 365.25 days (1 year)
  • ⏳ **Rotation Period**: 24 hours
  • 🌡️ **Surface Temperature**: -88°C to 58°C (-126°F to 136°F)
  • ☁️ **Atmosphere**: 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen, 1% other gases
  • 🌕 **Moon**: 1 (The Moon)

🚀 Space Exploration of Earth

  • 🛰️ **International Space Station (ISS)** – Orbiting Earth since 1998
  • 🛰️ **Hubble Space Telescope** – Observing deep space from Earth's orbit
  • 🛰️ **Landsat Missions** – Studying Earth's surface and climate
  • 🛰️ **Upcoming Missions**: Advancements in climate monitoring and space tourism
