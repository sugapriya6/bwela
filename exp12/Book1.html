Story Collector

About the Book:

  • Name : The Story Collector
  • Author : Kristin O'Donnell Tubb
  • Publication Date: Approximately 6.5 years ago
  • Publisher : Henry Holt & Company

Synopsis:

Inspired by the real-life Viviani Joffre Fedeler, this book takes place in the 1920s inside the New York Public Library, where Viviani has grown up. She is a natural storyteller, always weaving tales for her friends. However, when a new girl at school challenges her to prove that her stories are real, Viviani and her friends embark on a thrilling mystery adventure. Rumors of a ghost haunting the library push them into an investigation, leading to unexpected discoveries about the power of stories and friendship.

Themes:

  • The magic of libraries
  • Childhood imagination and mystery
  • The importance of truth in storytelling
