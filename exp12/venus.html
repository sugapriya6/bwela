🌟 Venus - The Hottest Planet

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is often called Earth's "sister planet" due to its similar size and composition. However, Venus has a **thick, toxic atmosphere** made of carbon dioxide, which traps heat and makes it the hottest planet in our solar system, with surface temperatures reaching **900°F (475°C)**!

🌌 Key Facts About Venus

  • 🌞 **Orbit Duration**: 225 Earth days
  • ⏳ **Rotation Period**: 243 Earth days (longer than its year!)
  • 🔥 **Surface Temperature**: ~900°F (475°C)
  • ☁️ **Atmosphere**: Mostly carbon dioxide with sulfuric acid clouds
  • 🔄 **Rotation Direction**: Retrograde (spins opposite to most planets)

🚀 Space Missions to Venus

  • 🛰️ **Venera Missions** (USSR) – First landers on Venus
  • 🛰️ **Magellan** (NASA) – Mapped 98% of Venus' surface
  • 🛰️ **Akatsuki** (JAXA) – Studying Venus’ atmosphere
  • 🛰️ **Upcoming Mission**: NASA's **VERITAS** (2030s) to explore Venus in detail
