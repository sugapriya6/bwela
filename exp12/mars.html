Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is known as the **Red Planet** due to its iron-rich soil that gives it a reddish appearance. It has **the largest volcano in the solar system (Olympus Mons)** and **a canyon system (Valles Marineris) that is over 4,000 km long**. Mars has **thin air mostly made of carbon dioxide** and **freezing temperatures** but is a top candidate for future human colonization.
