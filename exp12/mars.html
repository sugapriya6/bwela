🪐 Mars - The Red Planet

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is known as the **Red Planet** due to its iron-rich soil that gives it a reddish appearance. It has **the largest volcano in the solar system (Olympus Mons)** and **a canyon system (Valles Marineris) that is over 4,000 km long**. Mars has **thin air mostly made of carbon dioxide** and **freezing temperatures** but is a top candidate for future human colonization.

🌌 Key Facts About Mars

  • 🌞 **Orbit Duration**: 687 Earth days
  • ⏳ **Rotation Period**: 24.6 hours (similar to Earth)
  • ❄️ **Surface Temperature**: -87°C to -5°C (-125°F to 23°F)
  • ☁️ **Atmosphere**: 95% carbon dioxide, 3% nitrogen, 1.6% argon
  • 🌕 **Moons**: 2 (Phobos & Deimos)

🚀 Space Exploration of Mars

  • 🛰️ **Viking Missions** (NASA, 1976) – First successful landers on Mars
  • 🤖 **Curiosity Rover** (2012) – Studying Mars' geology and climate
  • 🤖 **Perseverance Rover** (2021) – Searching for signs of ancient life
  • 🚀 **Future Mission**: NASA and SpaceX plan human missions in the 2030s
