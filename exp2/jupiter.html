Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system. It is a gas giant with a mass more than two and a half times that of all the other planets combined. It is known for its prominent Great Red Spot and at least 79 moons, including the four largest: Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto.

Atmosphere: Jupiter is a gas giant with a thick atmosphere of hydrogen and helium.

Water/Ice: It has no liquid water, but some moons like Europa may have oceans beneath their ice.

Other Facts: Famous for the Great Red Spot, a giant storm. Contains powerful magnetic fields. Has at least 79 moons and faint rings.
