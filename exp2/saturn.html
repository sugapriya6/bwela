Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and is known for its stunning ring system. The rings are made of ice and rock particles. Saturn has more than 80 moons, with Titan being the largest and of particular interest due to its thick atmosphere and lakes of methane.

Atmosphere: Saturn is a gas giant with an atmosphere mostly of hydrogen and helium.

Water/Ice: No liquid water on Saturn itself, but some moons may harbor water ice.

Other Facts: Its spectacular rings are made of ice and rock. Titan, its largest moon, has lakes of liquid methane. Saturn has a low density, less than water.
