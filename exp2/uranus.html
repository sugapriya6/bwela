Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and is unique for rotating on its side. It has a pale blue color due to the methane in its atmosphere. Uranus has 27 known moons and faint rings. It is a cold and distant ice giant.

Atmosphere: Uranus is an ice giant with an atmosphere containing hydrogen, helium, and methane.

Water/Ice: Water exists as ice deep inside the planet.
